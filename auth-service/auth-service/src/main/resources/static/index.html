About Alumni Connect

Alumni Connect is a simple, secure platform that helps you:

  • Find and connect with alumni mentors in your field.
  • Share internships, jobs, and milestones with your network.
  • Join topic-based groups and participate in community events.
  • Give back by guiding students through real-world insights.

Whether you're a current student looking for direction or an alumnus eager to help, Alumni Connect makes it easy to stay involved and make an impact.
